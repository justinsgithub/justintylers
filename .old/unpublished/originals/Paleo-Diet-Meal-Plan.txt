Paleo Diet foods to eat 
    - Meat (beef, lamb, goat, venison, moose)
    - Seafood (salmon, tuna, mackeral, catfish, haddock, cod, trout, anchovies)
    - Eggs (including egg yolks, known as "natures multivitamin")
    - Vegetables (bell peppers, squash, zucchini, arugula, cauliflower, broccoli, kale, onions, garlic, spinach)
    - Fats (olive oil, flaxseed oil, coconut oil, palm oil, avocado oil, olives, avocados)
    - Herbs and Spices (cumin, oregano, basil, pepper, ginger, tumeric, thyme, rosemary)
    - Seeds (hemp seeds, flax seeds, pumpkin seeds, chia seeds)
    - Nuts (cashews, Brazil nuts, macadamia nuts, pistachios, almonds, walnuts)
    - Fruits (grapes, strawberries, apples, bananas, orange, plums, peaches, melons, blueberries, rasberries, watermelon)
    - Poultry (chicken, turkey, goose, duck)

Paleo Diet foods not to eat
    - Legumes (beans, chickpeas, lentils, peanuts)
    - Grains (bread, pasta, rice, quinoa, farro, buckwheat, rye, barley)
    - Refined vegetable oils (grapeseed oil, cottonseed oil, soybean oil, cottonseed oil, canola oil, safflower oil)
    - Artificial sweeteners (sucralose, aspartame, potassium, acesulfame, saccharin)
    - Added sugars (baked goods, table sugar, sugar-sweetened beverages, desserts, candies)
    - Processed foods (chips, pretzels, fast food, convenience meals, cookies, )
    - Potatoes (potato chips, french fries, white potatoes)
    - Dairy (milk, yogurt, butter, kefir, cheese)

Sample Meal Plan (3 days)

Day 1 
    - Breakfast (chia pudding with coconut milk, walnuts, strawberries, blueberries, and cinnamon)
    - Lunch (bison burger with lettuce wrap and side salad)
    - Dinner (grilled chicken with vegetable soup)

Day 2 
    - Breakfast (grain-free granola with almonds, walnuts, pecans, coconut, flakes, and dried fruit)
    - Lunch (avocado and veggie egg salad and mixed fruit)
    - Dinner (burrito bowl with cauliflower rice, beef, salsa, guacamole, peppers, and onions)

Day 3 
    - Breakfast (omelet with garlic, onions, tomatoes, and spinach)
    - Lunch (zucchini noodles with turkey meatballs and mariana sauce)
    - Dinner (oven-baked salmon with roasted broccoli and sweet potato wedges)
